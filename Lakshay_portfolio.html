RAHI: Travelling Bus Booking Website

Problem:

The absence of a centralized bus booking and tracking platform in Haryana makes public transport planning difficult and inefficient for users.

Solution:

I'm Lakshay — a UI/UX designer focused on building clear, human-centered digital experiences.

Growing up, I've always been curious about why things feel confusing when they don't need to be. There's so much noise, overload, clunky systems, unclear instructions. And it's why great ideas fail because of poor execution. That curiosity led me to design — asking "why" and "why not."

Today, I work at the intersection of UX systems, typography, layout, and responsive design. I enjoy breaking complex ideas into simple, scalable structures — whether that's a landing page, a design system, or a concept prototype. I believe that design shouldn't leave you guessing — it should guide quietly and confidently.

I care deeply about clarity, fairness, and intent in design. Every decision matters — from spacing, hierarchy, interaction — should respect the user's time and attention. My goal is to build work that feels thoughtful, usable, and honest, while continuously learning and refining my craft.

My core Value:

"Clarity over complexity — in design, work, and life."

"Build with intent, empathy, and respect for people's time."

"Progress comes from consistency, not noise."
